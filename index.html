Our Mission

The Bible Plus is born from a passion to make profound biblical understanding accessible and free for everyone. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool to help individuals connect with Jesus and grow in their faith without financial barriers. Our goal is to provide a reliable, insightful, and user-friendly platform for Bible study.

We're starting this project with a small, dedicated team and the help of volunteers, committed to building a tool that truly serves the global Christian community.
